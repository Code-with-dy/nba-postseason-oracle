]]> NEW YORK – The NBA today announced the field for the 2026 State Farm 3-Point Contest on Saturday, Feb. 14 at Intuit Dome in Inglewood, Calif.  The competition is the first event of NBA All-Star Saturday, which tips off at 5 p.m. ET on NBC and Peacock.

The eight-player field, revealed on NBC, is as follows:

• Devin Booker, Phoenix Suns
• Kon Knueppel, Charlotte Hornets
• Damian Lillard, Portland Trail Blazers
• Tyrese Maxey, Philadelphia 76ers
• Donovan Mitchell, Cleveland Cavaliers
• Jamal Murray, Denver Nuggets
• Bobby Portis Jr., Milwaukee Bucks
• Norman Powell, Miami Heat

The State Farm 3-Point Contest features five 2026 NBA All-Stars: Booker, Maxey, Mitchell, Murray and Powell.

The field also includes two former State Farm 3-Point Contest winners, Booker (2018) and two-time champion Lillard (2023 and 2024).  Lillard, a nine-time NBA All-Star, can match the record of three titles, shared by Larry Bird and Craig Hodges.

Four players will make their State Farm 3-Point Contest debuts: Knueppel, Maxey, Murray and Portis.  Knueppel can become the first rookie to win the event.

In the two-round contest, players attempt to score as many points as possible from multiple 3-point locations within 70 seconds.  The top three scorers from the first round advance to the championship round, where the highest score determines the winner.

]]> NEW YORK – The NBA today announced the teams for the 2026 Kia Shooting Stars on Saturday, Feb. 14 at Intuit Dome in Inglewood, Calif.  The timed shooting competition returns to NBA All-Star Saturday, which tips off at 5 p.m. ET on NBC and Peacock.

Four teams of three – each featuring two NBA players and one NBA legend – will compete in the event.  The teams reflect NBA All-Star connections, school and team affiliations, and family ties.

• Team All-Star: Toronto Raptors guard-forward Scottie Barnes and Oklahoma City Thunder forward-center Chet Holmgren, 2026 NBA All-Star Game teammates, are joined by three-time NBA All-Star Richard Hamilton.

• Team Cameron: Three Duke University alums who starred at Cameron Indoor Stadium team up – 2026 NBA All-Star Jalen Johnson of the Atlanta Hawks, Castrol Rising Star Kon Knueppel of the Charlotte Hornets and former 14-year NBA veteran Corey Maggette.

• Team Harper: Five-time NBA champion Ron Harper Sr. pairs up with his sons, San Antonio Spurs guard Dylan Harper and Boston Celtics guard-forward Ron Harper Jr.

• Team Knicks: New York Knicks teammates Jalen Brunson and Karl-Anthony Towns, both 2026 NBA All-Stars, compete with Allan Houston, who made two NBA All-Star teams with New York and now serves as its Vice President of Player Leadership & Development.

Kia Shooting Stars will feature a two-round format, with all four teams competing in the first round and the top two advancing to the final round.  Teams will compete one at a time and have 1:10 to score points while rotating through seven designated shooting locations around the court, with all three players on a team shooting at each spot in a set order.  The team with the higher score in the final round will be crowned the champion.

]]> LOS ANGELES – The NBA today unveiled the uniforms and court for the 75th NBA All-Star Game on Sunday, Feb. 15 at Intuit Dome in Inglewood, Calif., home of the LA Clippers. The annual midseason showcase will air on NBC/Peacock at 5 p.m. ET/2 p.m. PT in the United States and reach fans in more than 200 countries and territories in more than 50 languages.

Drawing inspiration from the city of Los Angeles and the new USA vs. World format, the NBA All-Star court and uniforms celebrate the city’s rich basketball history and the league’s global influence. For the ninth consecutive year, Jordan Brand will outfit the stars in Sunday’s game. The court will be used across NBA All-Star events at the state-of-the-art Intuit Dome and Kia Forum.

2026 All-Star Game Uniform Design Details
The uniforms reflect the 2026 NBA All-Star Game format featuring two teams of USA players and one team of international players. A Los Angeles-inspired script spells out “All-Stars” across the chest of all jerseys, while seven stars surrounding the wordmarks pay tribute to the seven previous times L.A. has hosted the game.

USA Uniforms:
• Uniquely celebrating the design of the American Flag, both uniforms emphasize the stars and stripes in a red, white and blue color palette.
• The USA STRIPES uniform incorporates a blue base color with a bold striped silhouette across the body, with red and white striped inserts along the right side of the jersey and shorts.
• The USA STARS uniform conversely features a red base color with a star silhouette showcasing blue and white stars along the right side of the jersey and shorts.

World Uniforms:
• The World All-Star team will wear a white uniform with blue and green paneling along the right side that represents the vastness of land and sea across the globe.

Shared Features Across All Uniforms:
• NBA Star graphics appear on each uniform on the front of the jersey and the side of the shorts incorporating team-specific motifs: stripes, stars, and a globe/compass design.
• Each jersey displays the player’s country flag on the back above the number.
• The jock tag commemorates the 75th annual All-Star Game.

The Jordan Brand Swingman NBA All-Star jerseys will be available for purchase on NBAStore.com and Nike.com on Feb. 4 at 10 a.m. ET.

NBA All-Star 2026 Court Design Details
• Alongside the NBA All-Star 2026 brand identity, the court design draws inspiration from the Los Angeles Clippers and the architectural structure of the Intuit dome.
• The court is designed in the Clippers’ color palette of navy blue, red, and pacific blue which flood the sidelines and baselines.
• Various shades of wood stain cover the playing surface, accented by nautical stars and triangular patterns derived from the Intuit Dome’s outer structure.
• The official NBA All-Star 2026 logo sits prominently at center court, which celebrates Los Angeles through its iconic palm trees.

New USA vs. World Format
As previously announced, The NBA All-Star Game will feature a USA vs. World format, with two U.S. teams and one World team competing in a round-robin tournament featuring four 12-minute games. This evolution underscores the NBA’s global reach and celebrates the diversity of talent across the league. More information on the 2026 NBA All-Star Game rosters can be found here.

]]> NEW YORK – LA Clippers forward Kawhi Leonard has been named by NBA Commissioner Adam Silver as an addition to the U.S. player pool for the 2026 NBA All-Star Game, which will take place on Sunday, Feb. 15 at 5 p.m. ET at Intuit Dome in Inglewood, Calif., and air on NBC and Peacock.

The 75th NBA All-Star Game will feature two teams of U.S. players and one team of international players, with each team consisting of at least eight players.  After the selection of the initial 24 All-Stars, Leonard was added to bring the total number of U.S. All-Stars to 16, the minimum required under the format.

Leonard is averaging a career-high 27.6 points (seventh in the NBA), 6.1 rebounds, 3.6 assists and a league-leading 2.06 steals in 36 games.  He is shooting 49.7% from the field, 39.1% from three-point range and 91.3% from the free throw line (fourth in the NBA).  Leonard has scored at least 20 points in each of his last 28 games, highlighted by a career-high 55-point performance against the Detroit Pistons on Dec. 28.

A 14-year NBA veteran, Leonard has earned his seventh All-Star selection.  In 2020, he received the Kobe Bryant Trophy as the NBA All-Star Game MVP.

The three NBA All-Star Game teams will be revealed tonight on Peacock, with the pregame show beginning at 7 p.m. ET, ahead of the matchup between the Boston Celtics and Dallas Mavericks at 8 p.m. ET on NBC and Peacock.
