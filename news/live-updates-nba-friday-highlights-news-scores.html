Clippers seize momentum, survive final push

Clippers 114, Kings 111

Kawhi Leonard finished with a game-high 31 points (9-19 FG, 12-12 FT), tacking on nine rebounds, seven assists and two steals as well. John Collins outpaced all Kings with 22 points (8-12 FG).

Stars shining bright, New Orleans tops Minnesota‚≠ê‚≠ê

Pelicans 119, Timberwolves 115

After trailing by as many as 18 points as late as Q3, New Orleans emerges victorious.

Anthony Edwards (35 points, 11-22 FG, 8-9 FT) put on a shot-making clinic, but Saddiq Bey (30 points, 10-21 FG) and Zion Williamson (29 points, 11-13 FG) set the tone with physical bucket-getting inside. Bey truly anchored the Pelicans’ performance on both ends.

New Orleans rained in 44.1% of 34 three-point attempts, while Minnesota settled perhaps, converting just 49.1% of their attempts from inside the arc.
