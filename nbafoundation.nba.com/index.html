Driving Economic Opportunity for Youth

The NBA Foundation invests in programs, partnerships and making grants to local and national organizations that promote school-to-career employment opportunities, including job readiness, skill training, job placement, and career advancement.

Learn about our mission

The Impact

The NBA Foundation is making an impact by supporting organizations that provide skills training, mentorship, professional coaching and pipeline development to foster employment and career advancement for under resourced youth.

877,000 youth served

877,000 youth have been served via our nonprofit partners and our signature programs that support school to career pipeline opportunities.

346 Nonprofit partners

Our nonprofit partners are opening career doors for under-resourced youth across diverse industries such as Arts, Criminal Justice, Healthcare, Music, STEM, Sports, and more.

$160M Awarded

The NBA Foundation awards grants to nonprofit organizations dedicated to serving under-resourced youth across the United States and Canada.
